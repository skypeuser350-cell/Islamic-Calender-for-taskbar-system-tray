‚ú® Key Functions & Features

  • **System Tray Integration:** Sits silently next to your clock, speaker, and Wi-Fi icons.
  • **Configurable Offset:** Right-click the icon to adjust the date by **-1 day** (for the Indian calendar) or **+1 day**.
  • **Lightweight:** Does not require Python to be installed on your computer.
  • **Basic GUI:** Provides a simple Windows-style pop-up when clicked to view the full date.
